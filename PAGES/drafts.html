Doug Guide

Drafting (From Hardstuck Legendary so its kinda scuffed)

If you want to have a good match to win, rather than play him for the fun of playing him, heres just a quick rundown

When to pick:

  1. Closed/Bushy Maps
  2. Low DPS Enemies
  3. When all the good anti tanks are banned
  4. Need someone that heals

When not to pick:

  1. High DPS Enemies
  2. Anti-tanks are open
  3. Open map -- unless your confident
  4. Heist
  5. Squish Teammates (unless you wanna play bodyguard Doug)
  6. Tank Enemies (Unless they're going to be bunched up)
